We are fully aware of the recent controversies that came as a result of Starbucks only accepting Campus Dirhams. This is not a university policy to limit the consumption of this psychoactive drug, or an attack to the student body we gladly serve to everyday, but a last resource economic lifeline agreed by ADNH and NYUAD.

This decision is in effect for the time being, but it is our utmost priority to continually update our offerings in benefit of the NYUAD community as a whole. In light of this sentiment, we have participated in the student-run 30 minute film festival as a way to strengthen the connection we have with students on campus.

30 Minute Felm Festival

In this short documentary, we went around NYUAD to ask our students what will they eat for lunch. We are happy to know that students are benefiting from the healthy, nutritious, and invigorating options we gladly serve on a daily basis.

This was a fun experience, and we hope we continue recording memories of students enjoying D2. Expect more to come!
